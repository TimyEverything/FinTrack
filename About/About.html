About Us

We are Finance Tracker, a web-based platform that helps you manage your personal finances with ease and efficiency. Whether you want to track your income and expenses, set and achieve your financial goals, or plan for your retirement, we have the tools and features that you need.

Our mission is to empower you to take control of your money and achieve financial freedom. We believe that everyone deserves to have access to reliable and user-friendly financial tools that can help them make smart decisions and improve their financial well-being

We are a team of passionate and experienced professionals who have been working in the finance industry for over a decade. We have seen the challenges and frustrations that people face when it comes to managing their money, and we wanted to create a solution that would make it easier and more enjoyable.

That’s why we created Finance Tracker, a web site that combines powerful functionality with simple and intuitive design. With Finance Tracker, you can:

  • • Connect your bank accounts and credit cards and see all your transactions in one place.
  • • Categorize your income and expenses and get insights into your spending habits and patterns.
  • • Create budgets and track your progress towards your financial goals.
  • • Generate reports and charts that show your income, expenses, net worth, cash flow, and more.
  • • Plan for your future with our retirement calculator and savings planner.
  • • Access your financial data anytime, anywhere, from any device.

We are constantly working to improve our web site and add new features and functionalities that will make your financial journey easier and more rewarding. We value your feedback and suggestions, and we are always happy to hear from you

If you have any questions, comments, or concerns, please feel free to contact us at click-desk.champions@fintrack.com. We would love to hear from you and help you with any issues you may have.

Thank you for choosing Finance Tracker, the ultimate web site for your personal finance needs. We hope you enjoy using our web site and achieve your financial goals.
